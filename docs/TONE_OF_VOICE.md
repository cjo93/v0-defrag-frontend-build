# TONE OF VOICE GUIDELINES

DEFRAG is built on a foundation of clarity, calm, and grounded understanding. The tone of voice across all copy, UI elements, and AI responses must reflect these principles.

## Core Principles

- **Human-first**: Center the human experience. Speak directly and respectfully.
- **Calm clarity**: Use simple, straightforward language. Avoid hype, urgency, or artificial excitement.
- **No urgency manipulation**: Never pressure the user. Avoid "do this now" or "act fast" framing.
- **No diagnostic tone**: Do not pathologize or use clinical language (e.g., "toxic", "narcissist", "disorder").
- **No authority positioning**: DEFRAG observes patterns; it does not dictate truth or tell the user what they "must" do.
- **No optimization framing**: Avoid treating human relationships like a machine to be "fixed" or "optimized."
- **No productivity framing**: Do not frame relational understanding as a "hack" or a way to "level up."

## Banned Language Categories

The following words and concepts are strictly forbidden in user-facing copy:

- "System"
- "Engine"
- "Optimize"
- "Processing"
- "Performance enhancement"
- "Execute"
- "Deploy your mental stack"
- Any mechanical metaphors (e.g., "recalibrate", "reboot", "gears")
- Any clinical diagnosis language (e.g., "trauma", "trigger", "codependent")
- Any fear-based persuasion or urgency triggers.

## Approved Language Patterns

Use these words to encourage reflection and gentle observation:

- "Notice"
- "Reflect"
- "Pause"
- "Explore"
- "Consider"
- "Support"
- "Clarity"
- "Grounded"

## Sentence Structure Constraints

- Keep sentences short.
- Be clear and use plain language.
- Avoid jargon, buzzwords, and complex clauses.
- Aim for a 6th–8th grade reading level.
- One idea per sentence.
